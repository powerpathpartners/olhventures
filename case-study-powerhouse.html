The Challenge

Hyperscale Demand Meets Market Headwinds

PowerHouse Data Centers needed to build hyperscale lease momentum across multiple U.S. campuses at a time when record land costs and transmission constraints were slowing the entire market. The commercial challenge was clear: attract and close long-term hyperscale commitments at scale, across multiple geographies, in a compressed timeline.

Our Approach

Positioning, Negotiation & Structured Execution

  • Defined campus positioning and target off-taker profiles tailored to each market's power, connectivity, and permitting advantages
  • Led direct negotiations with hyperscalers for long-term capacity commitments, leveraging 30+ years of established relationships
  • Structured power allocations and lease terms optimized for multi-phase scalability, aligning developer economics with tenant requirements
Results

Market-Defining Outcomes

  • 3 GW leased in 24 months — one of the largest leasing totals in the U.S.
  • ~$3 million/month in contracted revenue across a 15-year term
  • Established PowerHouse as a premier hyperscale landlord with sustained expansion momentum
